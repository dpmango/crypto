Multi-factor auth

We recommend enabling multi-factor authentication (MFA) for your account.

MFA adds a level of security to your account by requiring physical access to a second device under your control. Using a mobile app like Google Authenticator your device will generate temporary passwords which will be required each time you log in.

To enable multi-factor auth

  1. Scan the QR code in Google Authenticator
  2. Enter the generated 6-digit passcode below
